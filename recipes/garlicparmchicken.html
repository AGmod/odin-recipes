Garlic Parmesan Chicken

Description

Garlic Parmesan Chicken is so delicious and a very easy recipe to make at home. The process from start to finish should take around 45 minutes to 1 hour.

Ingredients

  • 1-2lbs of chicken breast or chicken thighs
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon pepper
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • Buffalo Wild Wings garlic parmesan Sauce
