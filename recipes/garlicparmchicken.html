Garlic Parmesan Chicken

Description

Garlic Parmesan Chicken is so delicious and a very easy recipe to make at home. The process from start to finish should take around 45 minutes to 1 hour.

Ingredients

  • 1-2lbs of chicken breast or chicken thighs
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon pepper
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • Buffalo Wild Wings garlic parmesan Sauce

Steps

  1. Grab your chicken breats or chicken thighs and clean them under running cold water.
  2. Put the thighs in a bowl to prepare them for seasoning.
  3. Add 1 tbsn of each seasoning(salt,pepper,garlic power, onion powder, paprika)
  4. Grab the garlic parmesan sauce from Buffalo Wild Wings and pour it onto the chicken.
  5. Mix everything together until the chicekn is full coated, then cover it with plastic and let it marinate in the fridge for atleast 1 hour.
  6. Get a flat oven pan and spread your marinated chicken all over and preheat the oven to 400 degrees.
  7. Slide chicken tray in over and let cook for about 45-50 minutes or until internal chicekn temp is 175 degrees.
  8. Once chicken is cooked, you can also broil it for an additional 10 minutes to give it a crispy crust.
  9. Your Garlic Parmesan chicken is ready for you to enjoy!
