Burger

Description

This is a burger recipe to make my personal favorite entree to make at home.

It will be so delicious you will rather make it at home instead of ordering from a restaurant!

Ingrediants

  • Sesame seed bun
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • American Cheese
  • Ground beef or beef patty
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayo
  • The Blend seasoning

Steps

  1. Start by slicing your tomatos, onions, and lettuce to prep for the burger(s).
  2. Mix a table spoon of ketchup, mustard, and mayo to create the spread for the burger.
  3. Once the first two steps have been finished, heat up a pan on the low-end of medium head and add a tablespoon of your preferred vegetable oil.
  4. Once the pain is ready, grab your beef patty and place gently on the pan. While the meat is cooking, add some of The Blend seasoning and some mustard to the top side of the patty.
  5. As soon as the patty starts to look a bit sweaty from the top, flip over the patty and you can add a little more seasoning to this side as well.
  6. Once the meat looks cooked to your liking, you can add American cheese to the patty and cover the pan for about 30 seconds to 1 minute.
  7. While the meat cooks, you can go ahead and toast the bread as well.
  8. Then, place your toasted buns on a plate and add the sauce, lettuce, tomatos, and onions to the top half of the bun.
  9. Finally, add the cooked patty to the bottom bun and voila! You cooked yourself a delicious burger!
