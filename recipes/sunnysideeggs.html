Sunny Side Up Eggs

Description

Sunny side up eggs are a staples in breakfast cuisine that is loved by many. You can pair these eggs with bacon, hashbrowns, rice, etc.

Ingredients

  • 1 to 2 eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Prefferred cooking oil

Steps

  1. Heat up a nonstick pan on low/medium heat.
  2. Crack one to two eggs on to heated pan while adding salt and pepper to each egg.
  3. Grab a lid and place over pan, leaving it for around 3-4 minutes.
  4. Cook the eggs until the clear part turns milky color while the yolk still looks runny.
  5. Thats it! You made yourself some sunny side up eggs!
